Welcome to my first website created using HTML!
My name is Kimberly Evans, and I have been a music teacher in Kentucky for the past 18 years. I have experience teaching at both the middle and elementary school levels. I earned both my bachelor's and master's degrees from Eastern Kentucky University (EKU), and I am currently pursuing my Rank 1 certification in School Library Media at EKU. In addition to teaching music, I also serve as one of the STLP (Student Technology Leadership Program) coaches at my school. This role has provided a rewarding opportunity to engage with students in a new and exciting way beyond the traditional classroom.


My hope with this site is to provide some of my favorite resources throughout my 18 years of teaching for others to enjoy.


I hope you find the resources I have provided on this site helpful.
